The flower of life, which is widely used in all monotheistic religions, tells the perfection of creation and connects us to this energy.

This symbol, which includes the principle of unity despite the difference, enables us to find our place in the web of life and to start innovations easily. The flower of life, which hides the secret of divine abundance, keeps us in touch with our essence and illuminates our way with its light. This symbol, which expresses that everything in life has a single creative source and that everything that exists is related to each other, opens new doors on our way and liberates us with each new step. The symbol of innovation, the flower of life, brings us together with new connections in harmony. When we carry this symbol or meditate with it, every 'new' person, culture, belief that we come across turns into a new opportunity; enriches and strengthens us materially and spiritually. The feelings of gratitude and gratitude in our souls increase and we begin to make new decisions in life with ease.
