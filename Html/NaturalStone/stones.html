AQUAMARINE STONE BENEFITS:

Aquamarine is known as the stone of courage. The most well-known and beautiful varieties are found in Russia. It means "sea water" in Latin and the stone is believed to be a treasure of mermaids. In addition, since ancient times, aquamarine has been known as the secret of sailors' safe voyage. In other words, sailors would definitely take aquamarine stone with them when they set out.
